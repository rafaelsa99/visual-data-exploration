Placed Students' Grades

The visualization presents the time evolution of the application grades of the placed students in a given course, as well as the distribution of the placed students among the several grades.
The mean of the different years is presented for each grade, being represented by the line in the graphic.
  • Interactions
  • Conclusions
The interactions available on the visualization are:

Time range selection: Through the slider it is possible to select a specific time range, within the available years, to present the information.

Course selection: Through the drop-down list it is possible to choose the course for which the information is to be seen.

Data filtering: By pressing the several lines of the caption, presented next to the graphic, it is possible to show/hide information regarding a year or the mean.The information visible in the graphic is represented with their caption in bold.

Detailed information: By moving the cursor to a bar of a specific grade, it is possible to see detailed information about the year and the number of placed students in that year with that grade.
