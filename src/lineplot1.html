Last Placed Students' Grades

The visualization presents the time evolution of the admission grades at MIEET and similar courses.
  • Interactions
  • Conclusions
The interactions available on the visualization are:

Time range selection: Through the slider it is possible to select a specific time range, within the available years, to present the information.

Data filtering: By pressing on the several courses, presented in the caption above the graphic, it is possible to show/hide its information. The courses visible in the graphic are represented with their caption in bold.

Brushing and detailed information: By moving the cursor to the line related to a course, it is possible to view detailed information about the admission grade in a specific year, highlighting that line in relation to the others.
