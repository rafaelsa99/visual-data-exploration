Students' Alternatives

The visualization presents the placements and alternatives of the candidates to MIEET and similar courses, considering all the options of the application or only one in specific.

The size of one fragment of the outer part of the diagram represents the percentage of applications for the course in relation to the total number of applications.
Examples of interpretation of chords:
UA-EET → UP-EEC: 4.52%: This means that 4.52% of the total number of applications, applied for the UA-EET course and had as an option the UP-EEC course.
UA-EET → UA-EET: 1.95%: This means that 1.95% of the total number of applications, applied for the UA-EET course and were placed.


  • Interactions
  • Conclusions
The interactions available on the visualization are:

Year selection: Through the slider it is possible to specify the year for which the information is to be viewed.

Option selection: Through the drop-down list it is possible to select the options of the applications that will be considered when extracting the information for the visualization.

Detailed information: By placing the cursor on a chord of the diagram it is possible to see detailed information about the percentage between the two entities.

Brushing: By placing the cursor on a fragment of the outer part of the diagram, the cords concerning that fragment are highlighted in relation to the others.
