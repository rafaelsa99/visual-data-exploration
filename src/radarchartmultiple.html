Preferences Option - Course

The visualization presents the preferences of the candidates regarding the course, depending on the option of the application.

  • Interactions
  • Conclusions
The interactions available on the visualization are:

Year selection: Through the slider it is possible to specify the year for which the information is to be viewed in each graphic.

Data filtering: By pressing on the course caption, presented next to the graphic, it is possible to show/hide its information. When the course is visible, its caption is presented in bold.

Brushing: By moving the cursor over an area, that area is highlighted and it is possible to see to which course it refers.
