Preferences Option - Course

The visualization presents the preferences of the candidates regarding the course, depending on the option of the application.

  • Interactions
  • Conclusions
The interactions available on the visualization are:

Year selection: Through the slider it is possible to specify the year for which the information is to be viewed.

Data filtering: By pressing on the several courses, presented in the caption next to the graphic, it is possible to show/hide its information. The courses visible in the graphic are represented with their caption in bold.

Brushing: By moving the cursor over an area, that area is highlighted in relation to the others, and it is possible to see to which course it refers.

Animation of the evolution: By clicking the button, it is possible to view an animation in the graphic, showing the data for each year between the selected time interval. The duration of the animation in each year can be adjusted in the input.

Time range selection for the animation: Through the slider with two variables it is possible to select a specific time interval for the animation of the evolution.
